Our Technology Stack

We expertise in these technologies to develop the perfect ChatBot of your needs.

Messenger Platform

Messenger Platform enables your business to handle queries from customers. It also helps you in leveraging ads to bring people to your chatbot experience.

Dialogflow

DialogFlow by Google is a chatbot development platform that provides with graphical interface to manage flow of chat conversation. It also supports voice assistants like Google Assitant and Amazon Alexa.

IBM Watson Assistant

IBM Watson Assistant is a question-and-answer system that knows when to find an answer from a knowledge database, hence, providing a dialogue interaction between the conversation system and users.

Amazon Lex

Amazon Lex processes voice or text input in conversation with your end-users and enables you to easily publish your voice/text chatbots to mobile devices, website applications and chat services.

Bot Framework

Microsoft Bot Framework enables developers to create, integrate and manage a wide range of bots and bot experiences. The tools available in this framework reduces the amount of code and coordination required to develop an 'enterprise-ready' bot.
